Come meet our troop, learn about our activities, and see how AHG helps girls grow in faith, character, and leadership!

In the coming year, our girls will be...

  • Camping, canoeing, and hiking!
  • Planning and hosting dances and parties!
  • Volunteering and contributing in community events!
  • Joining other Florida troops for regional activities!
  • Making friends, learning new skills, and much, MUCH MORE!

Our Core Values

  • Faith: Growing in our Christian faith and values
  • Service: Giving back to the community
  • Citizenship: Loving our country and honoring our heritage
  • Leadership: Building confidence and character
  • Integrity: Living out what we believe

Program Levels

  • Pathfinders: At least 5 years old
  • Tenderhearts: At least 6 years old
  • Explorers: At least 9 years old
  • Pioneers: At least 12 years old
  • Patriots: At least 14 years old
